Flick

Flick, a bug loving chameleon, is the newest host of Bug-Off, which is an annual event that occurs every third Saturday during the Summer months.

You can stand to make a ton of money by stockpiling rare bugs in preparation for Flick's appearance on your island.

Celeste

Celeste is an owl in the Animal Crossing series and is Blathers' younger sister. In previous games, she helped players identify and enjoy constellations, but her role in Animal Crossing: New Horizons has changed to informing players about shooting stars and meteor showers, as well as teaching them learn new DIY recipes for Wands and other items that incorporate Star Fragments and other materials gained from wishing on shooting stars..

Leif

Leif is a special character in Animal Crossing who is set to appear during this year's Earth Day event, scheduled to occur at the end of April. Leif has a similar role to Zipper during Bunny Day!
